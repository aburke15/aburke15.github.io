Performance Assessment - Task 1

Pet Paradigm Website Redesign Proposal

Objectives

  • Your first objective is to evaluate the existing website and user design specifications provided by Paradigm Pet Professionals (see Web Links and Supporting Documents sections). You will also develop a sitemap and wireframe for the redeveloped website.
  • Your second objective is to create an interactive desktop prototype of the redeveloped website according to the design specifications provided by Paradigm Pet Professionals.

A.1 - Functionality + Audience & Stakeholder Needs

  • Stakeholder Needs:
    • Pet Paradigm owners and stakeholders need a website that improves navigation and content clarity for users, offers basic pet care advice for common pet types, enables users to easily contact the company for consultations, and highlights the company's values to strengthen brand recognition.
  • Audience Needs:
    • Users of the Pet Paradigm website require easy navigation, content written in plain language for better understanding, accessibility features, and a responsive design to ensure optimal usability across various device screen sizes.
  • Proposed Microinteractions:
    • Ensure all images and buttons (navigation, links, sharing) have descriptive text for accessibility.
    • Display company contact information, including phone number and email, at the top and bottom of each page for easy access.
    • Include a hover animation on the navigation bar, along with an audio response when clicked, to improve interactive navigation.
    • Add social media sharing buttons on or just below the navigation bar to encourage content sharing.
    • Underline all hyperlinks to improve link visibility and usability.
    • Provide options to share or download article content and educational resources with a clearly visible button.
    • Implement a contact form with options for consultation requests. The form should provide animated and auditory feedback on submission, signaling success or failure. Unsuccessful submissions will trigger a visual alert, preventing form resubmission until errors are corrected. A “Schedule an Appointment” button and a direct link to the “About” page with contact information will be placed in the navigation bar and at the top of each footer.

A.2 - New Content & User Personas

  • Persona: Fish Owners
    • Fish owners require information about creating a safe, habitable environment for their fish. New content on a dedicated page would address care and maintenance for fresh and saltwater fish, advocating for the use of water testing kits, detailing safe water chemical levels, and providing access to consultations with “Pexperts.” This content is essential to help owners maintain a healthy aquatic habitat.
  • Persona: Reptile Owners
    • Reptile owners seek guidance on proper habitat setup, temperature regulation, and species-specific care. New content could include information on habitat essentials (such as humidity and UV lighting), diet recommendations, and tips for handling and health monitoring. Access to a “Pexpert” consultation for issues like tank setup or health concerns would also be valuable.
  • Persona: Bird Owners
    • Bird owners need information on proper cage size, enrichment, and diet to support their bird's health and well-being. New content could address habitat enrichment ideas, daily interaction tips, dietary needs, and common health indicators to monitor. The ability to consult a “Pexpert” on topics such as socialization or health check-ups would be highly beneficial.
  • Navigation Enhancements:
    • Redesigned website elements will make it easier for user owners to locate relevant information. Adding a links in a dropdown menu on the navigation bar enables quick access to pet-specific resources within 1-2 clicks. A “Schedule an Appointment” contact form allows pet owners to easily arrange consultations for additional services, and information. The responsive site design will ensure efficient, mobile-friendly access to pet care resources.

A.5 - Aligning with Audience & stakeholders

  • Stakeholder Needs:
    • To boost brand recognition and establish a consistent brand presence, stakeholders want the site to be optimized for search engines through relevant keywords and SEO-friendly design. A universal footer will be added to each webpage, including accessible links or buttons for the contact form and company information. An “About” page will be introduced, offering users insights into the company's mission, values, and purpose. This page will also showcase the company's slogan or brand value statement below the header text and integrate it thoughtfully throughout the site's content where appropriate.
    • Stakeholders share a similar need with their audience for an intuitive website experience, prioritizing easy navigation, clear content comprehension, and the addition of a user-friendly contact form for direct engagement with the company.
    • Stakeholders aim to deliver high-quality, relevant information on pet healthcare, which will lead to the creation of dedicated, informative content on each pet-specific page. This content will focus on educating pet owners on best practices and health tips tailored to various pet types.
  • Audience Needs:
    • To enhance content comprehension and accessibility, all written content will undergo review and reformatting in alignment with the US National Archives and Records Administration (NARA) guidelines for plain language. Each pet-specific page will feature a summary at the top, listing key recommendations to provide quick, easily accessible information. Additionally, microinteractions will be incorporated throughout the site, enhancing the user experience by adding subtle visual, auditory, and animated cues to elements like the navigation bar, images, hyperlinks, and contact information. Social media sharing options will also be integrated to encourage users to interact and share content with ease.
    • To address accessibility and engagement, a dedicated “Contact Us” page will be introduced, featuring an appointment scheduling form, enabling users to directly reach out to the business.
    • For optimal website responsiveness, the site will be developed to adapt seamlessly to various screen sizes, including desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. The page layouts will be structured with a clear information hierarchy, presenting the most critical and frequently accessed content or tools from top to bottom. This approach ensures users can easily find relevant information no matter the device they use, creating an inclusive, user-friendly experience.
    • To improve ease of navigation, the navigation bar will be redesigned and expanded, with content organized into specific categories labeled clearly by navigation link names. This structure will enable users to scan the navigation bar and efficiently locate the content or actions they seek, ensuring a smooth, self-directed experience.

A.6 - Primary and Secondary Navigation Elements

  • Navigation Elements:
    • Primary Navigation Elements:
      • Navigation Bar - The main navigation bar will offer quick access to essential sections of the website, ensuring users can easily reach key pages.
        • Links will be provided to primary pages like Home, About Us, Schedule an Appointment, and Pet Information.
        • Users should be able to access any page within 1-2 clicks, enhancing their browsing experience with minimal effort.
      • Footer - Designed to provide convenient access to company information.
        • A “Schedule an Appointment” button will be placed above the footer, linking directly to the contact form page.
        • The footer will also include a link to the “About” page, enabling users to access detailed company information and contact details.
      • Company Logo - Positioned at the top of each page, the company logo will act as a quick link back to the homepage when clicked, ensuring ease of navigation across the site.
    • Secondary Navigation Elements:
      • Top Pet Care Tips - Each pet-specific page will feature a “Top Pet Care Tips” section at the top, summarizing essential recommendations for quick, easy access to the most relevant advice.
      • Contact Form - The “Schedule an Appointment” link in the navigation bar will lead users directly to a contact form, streamlining the process of connecting with the company.
      • Search Feature - A search bar will be integrated into the navigation bar, allowing users to find content quickly without relying solely on the navigation structure or dropdown menus. This feature enhances accessibility and expedites content retrieval.
      • Dropdown Menu - A dropdown menu will appear when hovering over or clicking on the “Pet Information” link in the navigation bar, providing options if multiple pages fall under this primary category (as indicated in the sitemap).
    • Navigation Awareness Strategies:
      • Current Page Highlighting - A color change will indicate the user's current location on the navigation bar and dropdown menu, helping users stay oriented as they navigate.
      • Page Headings - Each pet-specific page will include a distinct heading to provide a clear indication of the user's location within the website.
      • Link Color Change - All links will change color once clicked, signaling which pages or content have already been accessed.
      • Titles and Subheadings - Organized use of titles and subheadings on each page will help structure content and guide the visitor through various sections efficiently.
  • Navigation Elements: Meeting Stakeholder Needs
    • The primary navigation elements outlined above address stakeholder needs for a streamlined and intuitive navigation experience. By offering a well-organized navigation bar, consistent footer, and company logo link at the top of every page, the design supports easy access to essential information and services, enhancing user satisfaction.
    • Secondary navigation elements, including the dropdown menu and summarized content sections, fulfill stakeholder needs for accessibility and user support. The dropdown menu offers additional pathways to detailed pet care information, while the contact form streamlines communication with the business. The “Top Pet Care Tips” summaries further enhance content accessibility and understanding, helping users quickly grasp key information.
    • The footer also strengthens brand recognition by providing consistent company contact information across the site. This setup not only improves functionality but also ensures users can conveniently reach out for services or inquiries, directly aligning with stakeholders' objectives for engagement and brand visibility.
  • Navigation Elements: Meeting Audience Needs
    • The primary navigation elements address audience needs by ensuring a seamless browsing experience. The structured navigation bar allows users to explore various pages effortlessly, while the footer provides easy access to company information, enhancing the usability of services offered. Additionally, the prominently placed company logo at the top of each page allows users to return to the homepage with ease, improving site navigation for all visitors.
    • Secondary navigation elements meet the audience's need for simplicity and accessibility. The dropdown menu enables users to navigate directly to specific content areas related to pet care. The contact form, accessible via the “Schedule an Appointment” link, fulfills the audience's need for convenient ways to contact the business. Moreover, the inclusion of a “Top Pet Care Tips” section at the beginning of each pet-specific page provides a concise, accessible summary, making it easier for users to locate and comprehend key information.
